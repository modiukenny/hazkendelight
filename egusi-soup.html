Nutrient Value & Benefits

A rich and hearty Nigerian soup made from ground melon seeds (egusi), cooked with a blend of palm oil, fresh vegetables, and assorted meats or fish. The soup is thickened with the egusi and flavored with traditional spices, creating a creamy texture and bold taste. Often paired with pounded yam, fufu, or semo, this beloved dish is a staple in Nigerian cuisine and loved for its satisfying, comforting flavor.
